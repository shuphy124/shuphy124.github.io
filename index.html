About me

I received the Ph.D. degree from Tokyo Institute of Technology, in 2019. I am currently a Postdoc Fellow in BIMSA, China. My main research interests include AdS/CFT correspondence, Integrability and Non-perturbative Quantum Field Theory.

Education

2014 - 2019: PhD in Physics,Tokyo Institute of Technology, Japan

2019 - 2021: Nordita Fellow at Nordic Institute for Theoretical Physics (Nordita), Sweden

Research
