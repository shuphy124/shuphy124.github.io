About me

I received my Ph.D. degree at Tokyo Institute of Technology in 2019. I am currently a Postdoc Fellow in BIMSA and YMSC in Tsinghua, China. My main research interests include AdS/CFT correspondence, Integrability and Non-perturbative Quantum Field Theory.

Education

2014 - 2019: PhD in Physics, Tokyo Institute of Technology, Japan

Work experience

2021 - Now: Postdoc at Beijing Institute of Mathematical Sciences and Applications (BIMSA) and Yau Mathematical Sciences Center (YMSC), Tsinghua University, China

2019 - 2021: Nordita Fellow at Nordic Institute for Theoretical Physics (Nordita), Sweden
