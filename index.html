About me

I received the B.S. degree from Tokyo Institute of Technology, in 2014, and the Ph.D. degree from Tokyo Institute of Technology, in 2019. I am currently a Postdoc Fellow in BIMSA, China. My main research interests include AdS/CFT correspondence, Integrability and Non-perturbative Quantum Field Theory.

Research
